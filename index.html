Our Electrical Services Process

A seamless journey from consultation to completion

01

Meeting The Client

An expert from our team will schedule an on-site meeting with you. They'll go over your electrical needs, must-haves, layout & design possibilities, and value addition to your property.

02

Design Phase

Our electrical engineers and designers will start planning your electrical system. We'll help in aligning your budget with installation plans.

03

Proposal & Contract

We provide a detailed proposal to ensure that our scope of work, specifications, and pricing aligns with your vision. No hidden costs.

04

Onboarding Process

We apply for building permits at the Town Building Dept and take care of all inspections during execution.

05

Work at Site

We assign a dedicated project manager to maintain communication with you throughout the work progress and warranty period.

06

Handing Over

We will create a detailed checklist together with you, after a joint visit to the site. Upon project completion, we hand over all warranties and documentation.
